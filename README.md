# Awesome-Insurance-Brokers
## Top Insurance Brokers & Open-Source Alternatives

A curated guide to leading **SaaS/cloud-hosted insurance brokerage platforms** (like Newfront, Embroker, Vouch, Lockton, Hub International) and their **open-source/self-hosted equivalents**. 

**Open-source solutions are emphasized** for transparency, customization, cost control, and independence in insurance management.

---

## SaaS / Cloud-Hosted Insurance Brokers

Popular platforms offering risk management, business insurance, employee benefits, and specialized brokerage services with modern tech stacks.

### Leading Options
- **[Newfront](https://newfront.com)** — Modern brokerage combining veteran expertise with data-driven insights for business insurance and benefits.
- **[Embroker](https://embroker.com)** — Tech-enabled broker focused on fast, digital experiences for startups and tech companies.
- **[Vouch Insurance](https://vouch.com)** — Specialized in tech and startup insurance with tailored policies and quick issuance.
- **[Lockton](https://lockton.com)** — World's largest independent insurance brokerage providing customized risk, benefits, and retirement solutions.<grok-card data-id="e28cb5" data-type="citation_card" data-plain-type="render_inline_citation" ></grok-card>
- **[Hub International](https://hubinternational.com)** — Major North American broker offering property & casualty, employee benefits, and risk management services.<grok-card data-id="1ded6d" data-type="citation_card" data-plain-type="render_inline_citation" ></grok-card>

**Other notables**: Marsh McLennan, Aon, Gallagher, and various regional/specialty brokers.

These platforms typically provide personalized advice, market access, claims support, and digital tools, often with commission-based or fee structures.

## Open-Source / Self-Hosted Alternatives

These tools enable building custom insurance management systems, policy administration, claims handling, and brokerage workflows. Perfect for agencies, MGAs, or self-managed operations seeking flexibility.

### Featured Projects

- **[Openkoda Insurance Policy Management](https://openkoda.com)** — AI-powered, highly customizable open-source platform for policy administration, ideal for brokers and agencies. No vendor lock-in.<grok-card data-id="0a05e0" data-type="citation_card" data-plain-type="render_inline_citation" ></grok-card>
  - Best for: Comprehensive policy management with customization.

- **[Open Insure](https://github.com/daxaxelrod/open_insure)** — Open-source platform to organize, manage self-insurance policies, and handle claims.<grok-card data-id="ec22b5" data-type="citation_card" data-plain-type="render_inline_citation" ></grok-card>

- **[Open Insurance Platform](https://github.com/aposin/openinsuranceplatform)** — Comprehensive core insurance system supporting all lines of business (property, life, health, etc.), contracts, claims, and more.<grok-card data-id="432c3c" data-type="citation_card" data-plain-type="render_inline_citation" ></grok-card>

### Additional Open-Source Tools
- Various policy administration and agency management solutions built on flexible frameworks (e.g., via Openkoda templates).
- Custom scripts and libraries for claims processing, quoting, and risk assessment.
- Integrations with open data standards for insurance (check awesome lists or GitHub topics for "insurance" and "policy-administration").

**Tip**: Start with **Openkoda** or **Open Insurance Platform** for full brokerage-like functionality. Extend with Python/R tools for analytics and automation.

## Comparison

| Aspect              | SaaS Brokers                          | Open-Source / Self-Hosted                  |
|---------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| **Cost**            | Commissions / service fees            | Free (only infra / maintenance)            |
| **Customization**   | Platform-limited features             | Full source code and workflow control      |
| **Transparency**    | Broker-mediated market access         | Auditable systems and data handling        |
| **Setup Effort**    | Quick onboarding                      | Requires self-hosting and configuration    |
| **Use Case**        | Businesses seeking expert advice      | Agencies, developers, cost-conscious ops   |

## Getting Started

1. Identify your needs (policy admin, claims, risk assessment, specific lines of business).
2. Explore **Openkoda** for broker-focused policy management.
3. Use **Open Insurance Platform** for a full core system.
4. Deploy via Docker or your preferred stack; integrate with existing CRM/ERP.
5. Ensure compliance with local insurance regulations when self-hosting.
